Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Data that stays on your device

Everything you create in Rooster is stored locally on your iPhone:

  • Alarms and settings — times, labels, sounds, snooze preferences and mission configuration.
  • Camera — the photo and QR missions use the camera only to confirm you're up. Images are processed on-device and never uploaded, stored or shared.
  • Motion — the steps and shake missions count your movement on-device only.
  • NFC — the identifier of your registered NFC tag is stored only on your phone.
  • Crash and performance diagnostics — collected by Apple's MetricKit and processed on your device.

If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

Purchases

Rooster Pro subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see your name, email address or payment details.

To know whether Pro is unlocked on your device, the app uses RevenueCat, which receives your App Store receipt and a randomly generated anonymous identifier — nothing that identifies you personally.

Anonymous analytics

To understand which features are used and make the app better, Rooster sends anonymous usage signals (for example "alarm armed" or "onboarding completed") to TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service. These signals contain no personal information, no identifiers that can be traced back to you, no alarm times and no alarm content. TelemetryDeck anonymizes all data before we see it, in aggregate form only.

What we don't do

  • No user accounts — we never ask for your name, email or phone number.
  • No advertising and no advertising SDKs.
  • No tracking across apps or websites (the app declares no tracking to Apple).
  • No selling or sharing of data with data brokers — ever.

Permissions the app asks for

Rooster requests permissions only when a feature needs them: alarm scheduling (so alarms ring through Silent and Do Not Disturb), notifications, camera, motion and NFC. Each permission is used solely for the alarm feature it belongs to, and you can change any of them in iOS Settings at any time.

Children

Rooster is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Because Rooster holds no personal data about you, there is usually nothing for us to access, correct or delete. If you would like the anonymous purchase identifier RevenueCat holds for your device deleted, or have any other privacy request, email us and we'll take care of it within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.
